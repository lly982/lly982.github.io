张杰（Jason Zhang），1982年12月20日出生于四川省成都市，中国流行男歌手、音乐。2004年，获得《我型我秀》全国总冠军并出道。2007年，获得《快乐男声》成都赛区冠军、总决赛第四名；随后，发行EP《最美的太阳》，拿下亚马逊年度唱片销量冠军。 2012年，在人民大会堂开启个人首轮巡演 2013年，首次参加央视春晚，演唱歌曲《给我你的爱》。2014年，获得第42届全美音乐奖年度国际艺人奖，成为首位获得此奖的华人歌手。2016年，开启“张杰我想世界巡回演唱会”，覆盖亚、欧、美、澳四个大洲，并成为首位在欧洲开个唱的内地歌手。2017年，获得中国金唱片奖最佳流行歌手奖；2018年，开启“张杰未·LIVE巡回演唱会”，两次刷新鸟巢单场演唱会票房纪录；推出个人第12张专辑《未·LIVE》；作为中国文艺工作者代表受邀出访朝鲜。2019年，参加新中国成立70周年文艺晚会演唱歌曲《天耀中华》、作为中国歌手代表亮相亚洲文明对话大会——亚洲嘉年华。2020年，参加纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战70周年文艺晚会。张杰热心公益慈善事业。2012年，出资创立北斗星空爱心基金。截至2024年，已在中国各地建立79间张杰音乐梦想教室，在中国西部地区搭建了数十所公益小学。

Jason ,born in Chengdu, Sichuan Province on December 20th, 1982, is a popular male singer and musician in China. In 2004, he won the national championship of "My Show" and made his debut. In 2007, he won the champion of Chengdu Division of Happy Boys and the fourth place in the finals. Subsequently, EP "The Most Beautiful Sun" was released, winning Amazon's annual record sales champion. In 2012, he started his first tour in the Great Hall of the People. In 2013, he participated in the CCTV Spring Festival Evening for the first time and sang the song "Give me your love". In 2014, he won the 42nd american music awards International Artist Award, becoming the first Chinese singer to win this award. In 2016, the "Jason I Want the World Concert Tour" was launched, covering four continents: Asia, Europe, the United States and Australia, and became the first mainland singer to sing in Europe. In 2017, he won the Best Pop Singer Award of China Gold Record Award; In 2018, the "Jason ·LIVE Tour Concert" was opened to refresh the box office record of the Bird's Nest single concert twice; Released his 12th album, Not Live; Invited to visit China as a representative of Korean literary and art workers. In 2019, he participated in the literary evening of the 70th anniversary of the founding of New China, sang the song "Dayao China", and appeared as a representative of China singers at the Asian Civilization Dialogue Conference-Asian Carnival. In 2020, he participated in a literary evening to commemorate the 70th anniversary of Chinese people's Volunteer Army's war to resist US aggression and aid Korea. Jason is enthusiastic about charity. In 2012, it funded the establishment of the Beidou Star Love Fund. By 2024, 79 Jason Music Dream Classrooms have been established in all parts of China, and dozens of public primary schools have been established in the western part of China.
